Data Analytics

QuantumBlack is a McKinsey Company which helps companies use data to drive decisions. It combines business experience, expertise in large-scale data analysis and visualisation, and advanced software engineering know-how to deliver results. Form aerospace to finance, QuantumBlack helps companies prototype, develop and deploy bespoke data science and data visualisation solutions to make better decisions.

Bloomberg is the world’s primary distributor of financial data and a top news provider of the 21st century. By using its dynamic network of data, ideas and analysis to solve clients’ problems every day, Bloomberg is relied on to deliver accurate, real-time business and market information that helps people make important financial decisions.

Financial Services

Goldman Sachs is a leading global investment banking, securities and investment management firm that provides a wide range of financial services including corporations, financial institutions, governments and individuals. With the culture values teamwork, client service and giving back to the communities, it has been described as the foundation of the firm’s success. Goldman Sachs maintains offices in all major financial centres around the world.

Morgan Stanley is a US based financial services company that through its subsidiaries and affiliates, advises, and originates, trades, manages and distributes capital for governments, institutions and individuals. With its four core values – putting clients first, doing the right thing, leading with exceptional ideas and giving back, Morgan Stanley is committed to maintaining the first-class service and high standard of excellence.

Credit Suisse is a leading global wealth manager with strong investment banking capabilities, its operation has a global reach and extends about 50 countries worldwide across mature and emerging markets. Credit Suisse partners across countries, divisions and regions to deliver holistic financial solutions to its clients, including innovative products and special tailored advice. As a global and inclusive community, Credit Suisse benefits from a diverse range of perspective to create value and drive results for its clients, shareholders and communities.

KPMG is a global network of professional firms providing Audit, Tax and Advisory services. With a worldwide presence, KPMG continues to build on its successes thanks to clear vision, defined values and above all, its people. Its industry focus help KPMG firms’ professionals develop a rich understanding of clients’ businesses and the insight, skills and resources required to address industry-specific issues and opportunities.

Santander is a leading financial services provider, offering a wide range of personal and commercial financial products and services, with around 15 million customers across their Retail and Corporate Businesses. With the target – to become one of the best banks in the UK to work for, Santander is transforming its business, creating a simply, personal and fair culture in a dynamic and creative environment.

Investec is a distinctive specialist bank and asset manager which provides financial products and services, imbued with a personal approach, to a select client base in three principal markets: the UK and Europe, Southern Africa and Asia-Pacific. Nowadays Investec is publicly traded in London and Johannesburg, its divisions include Corporate & Institutional Banking, Private Banking, Wealth & Investment, and Asset Management.

LSEG (London Stock Exchange Group) is an international markets infrastructure business. Its diversified global business focuses on capital formation, intellectual property and risk and balance sheet management. As a leading developer and operator of high performance technology solution, LSEG operates an open access model, offering choice and partnership to customers across all of its business.

PIMCO is one of the world’s premier fixed income investment manager. With the focus on excellence and its short term and long term track record have led institutions, financial advisors and millions of individual investors to entrust PIMCO with their assets. From preparing for retirement to funding higher education, investing the clients’ assets is a tremendous responsibility of PIMCO, and for that reason it works relentlessly to help these investors reach their goals.

Fidelity’s goal is to make financial expertise broadly accessible and effective in helping people live the lives they want. Offering investment management, retirement planning, portfolio guidance, brokerage and many other financial products, Fidelity managed to help investors to achieve better results. Innovative yet personal, compassionate yet responsible, grounded by a tireless work ethic, this is the heart of the Fidelity way.

Point72 Asset Management is a global firm led by Steven Cohen that invests in multiple asset classes and strategies worldwide. Resting on more than a quarter-century of investing experience, Point72 seeks to be the industry’s premier asset manager through delivering superior risk-adjusted returns, adhering to highest ethical standards, and offering the greatest opportunities to the industry’s brightest talent.

Amplify Trading is a financial futures trading company that leverages years of successful trading experience to develop professional traders. It continues to develop profitable traders by providing cutting edge technology and support to ensure traders, regardless novice or professional to achieve their targets. Investing in technology to help progress their own traders, Amplify now also works with the world’s largest financial firms to enhance capital markets learning and identify the market leaders of tomorrow.

Educational Partners

EduKATE provides Data Science and Software Development training, assessment and recruitment solutions for forward-thinking companies around the world. It is developed by Cambridge Spark and is supported by the UK’s government innovation agency. EduKATE integrates research in source code analysis, compilers and recommender systems. its purpose is to provide highly personalised code feedback and diagnostics to learners within an industry simulated environment.

WiserGrads is an award-winning creative and recruitment company which combines culture and performance to change the way people think about work. As a graduate recruitment agency based in the heart of London, WiserGrads have specialist consultants across a range of sectors to help graduates finding their dream positions at a company that suit them.

Axiom Tutoring aims to provide bespoke and ethical Economics tuition to undergraduate students, the company is passionate about teaching and determined to improve the high standards. Aiming to be the leading tuition provider for Economics, it also gives 20% of profits to charitable fund in Albania which helps students from disadvantageous family background or remote areas.
